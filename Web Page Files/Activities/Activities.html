Hiking

Embark on a journey through the picturesque landscapes of the Pacific Northwest. Explore rugged trails that lead to awe-inspiring views of the sea, majestic mountains, and lush valleys, creating an unforgettable hiking experience.

Kayaking

Embark on an unforgettable adventure as you paddle the pristine waters of Puget Sound. Keep your eyes peeled, for you might just find yourself face-to-face with our resident orca whales.

Scuba Diving

Descend beneath the waves to explore the hidden wonders of the Sound. Encounter playful sea otters, graceful harbor seals, mysterious octopuses, and a host of other aquatic marvels in their natural habitat.

Whale Watching

Set sail on an unforgettable voyage to witness the majestic marine life of Puget Sound and the Pacific. Depending on the season, you may spot humpback whales, gray whales, minke whales, and the awe-inspiring orca whales up close.
