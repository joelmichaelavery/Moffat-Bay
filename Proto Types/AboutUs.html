About Moffat Bay Lodge

Our Story

Moffat Bay Lodge is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest. With a history dating back over a century, we have been providing a unique and unforgettable experience for nature enthusiasts, adventurers, and those seeking tranquility.

Our Location

Located on the picturesque Joviedsa Island, our lodge offers breathtaking views of the sea, rugged mountains, and lush valleys. The surrounding natural beauty sets the stage for a wide range of outdoor activities and adventures.

Our Mission

At Moffat Bay Lodge, our mission is to create an oasis of serenity and adventure, where guests can connect with nature, explore the outdoors, and create lasting memories. We're dedicated to preserving the environment, providing exceptional hospitality, and sharing the wonders of this unique island.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to book a stay with us, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to make your stay at Moffat Bay Lodge an extraordinary experience.
